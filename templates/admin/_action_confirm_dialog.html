{# Generic action-confirmation dialog. Opened by `static/js/admin.js` from any `form[data-admin-confirm]` submit, populated from the form's data-confirm-title / data-confirm-body / data-confirm-label attributes, then the originating form is submitted natively on confirm. Distinct from the photo-removal dialog (#admin-remove-confirm), which carries a reason field. Spanish-only, like the rest of admin. #}
